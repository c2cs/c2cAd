"C2Cschools.com is Athletics Management"

Partner with C2Cschools.com today and gain access to athletic management software offering extreme customization and security, countless features, and support that rivals any in existence. C2Cschools.com can handle all of your needs and will do it better than anyone else. Some of C2Cschools.com's most popular features include:

Electronic Forms

Manage all your members' required forms with an electronic form and filing system.

  • Create a digital library of all your required forms and customize them to your specifications.
  • Have members receive, access, fill-out, and submit all forms electronically.
  • Customize filing rules that will automatically file incoming forms for you! Spend your time at work on the important stuff. Leave the filing to us.

School Calendars

Create and reference detailed school calendars.

  • Export to Microsoft Outlook.
  • Export to iCal.
  • Create and edit events from one place.

Association Reports

Customize and refer to multiple reports outlining critical information needed to monitor the many facets of your day-to-day business. Reports are available to associations, schools, and teams.

Custom Websites

As a c2cschools.com partner, you can offer each of your members a free custom website. Sites are easily customizable and offer a professional web presence to every group in your state. Our programmers have ensured that no one needs any coding experience in order to create stunning websites.

Team Management

Let your members manage themselves while under your complete control.

  • Approve rosters for members so they can track athletes.
  • Members will be able to track athlete eligibility within the system.
  • Members will create their own schedules.

Add Games / Events

Create shared events and contracts from one place.

  • Because C2Cschools.com uses shared database technology, when members enter their home games, opponents schedules are automatically filled in.
  • Competition contracts are also automatically created with the creation of new events.
  • All aspects of contract management are handled directly within the system. All responsible officials can access contracts in one place.

Venue Management

Create, manage, and share venues within the C2C system.

  • Members can create profiles for each of their venues.
  • Venues can be attached to created events.
  • Warning are issued each time a member attempts to double-book a venue.
  • Driving directions are made available to any person viewing an event.

Additional Event Information

Your members' users will be able to search for and find all the additional information they need concerning any scheduled events, such as:

  • Driving directions.
  • Weather forecasts.
  • Extended event participant information.

Bracketing

Create custom interactive brackets which display up to 64 teams on a single page. Interactive content provides access to participant school and event information.

  • Advertising options as well as coupon distribution available.
  • Automatic required forms emailed to advancing participants.
  • Brackets are editable in the case wrong scores are reported.
  • Brackets customizable for 16. 32, 64, and 128 teams.

Athlete Importing

Find simple and robust athlete importing options no matter what your data source. C2Cschools can even customize methods with which you can import athlete grades and other information if necessary.

  • Create and save import protocol for easy re-use.
  • Import grade data that will be automatically calculated for eligibility purposes.
  • Automate the import process via ftp methods.
  • Import athlete data for multiple schools at the same time.

Eligibility Tracking

C2C Schools' member associations can create custom global eligibility checks for all member schools, officials, coaches, and athletes.

  • Schools can create and enforce additional custom eligibility checks at a local level.
  • Association eligibility requirements will always override local requirements.
  • Athletes are color coded based on eligibility and interfaces are fully searchable and customizable.

Technical Support

Utilize un-matched technical support to ensure that you never have a moment of down-time.

  • Personal training for all member associations and schools through statewide seminars.
  • Access to an extensive Knowledgebase filled with articles, guides, and videos.
  • Create support tickets for one-on-one e-support.
  • Access to personal phone support @ (866) C2C-GAME or (866) 222-4263.

Advertising

Find advertising opportunities at both the association and school level as an alternative means of generating revenue. Advertisements in C2C use a rotating module with guaranteed impression software.

Some Other Benefits

There are many other benefits available within the C2C environment. Our developers are constantly adding and improving features in order to meet the needs of our members, such as:

  • The ability to create, print, and issue cards for Principals, Athletic Directors, Coaches, and Officials.
  • The ability to create polls and collect and analyze constituent voting information.
  • The ability to create and track Rules Presentations for different types of users.

Security

Security is one of our primary concerns. Our servers are housed in high security and armed locations and no wireless devices are used anywhere in our infrastructure.

  • We offer true 256 bit encrypted SSL.
  • We are PCI compliant with website assurance certificates.
  • All passwords are fully encrypted before they are stored and cannot be recovered if lost, only replaced.
  • User login originated IP addresses are stored in order to detect possible fraudulent login attempts.
  • We do not allow access to old and less secure browsers such as IE 7.0 and below.
